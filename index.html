"Spider-Man: Far From Home" is a superhero film that is part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe, depicting the adventures of the Spider-Man character. Directed by Jon Watts and starring Tom Holland, this film was released in 2019. The story is set in the aftermath of the events of "Avengers: Endgame." Peter Parker (Tom Holland) is struggling to fill the void left by Tony Stark's death and the absence of Iron Man. Desiring a vacation, Peter embarks on a European tour with his friends and schoolmates. However, this vacation plan is disrupted unexpectedly when Nick Fury (Samuel L. Jackson) reaches out for his help.
